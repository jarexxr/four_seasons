Summer

Likes

One the best thing about summer is the long days. It feels good to know that the sun doesn't set until 8:30pm. The freedom to enjoy the outdoors, spending time with friends and family doing a backyard BBQ. Driving with your window down is great feeling. Everything feels more alive.

Dislikes

I hate it when there a heat wave coming in. It's hard to anything outside when the sun is beating down on you. Bugs are the absolute worse. Waking up the next day after enjoying outdoor BBQ only to find out my leg been bitten by mosquitos a few times. Sleeping is near impossible if don't have anything to cool your room.

Autumn

Likes

Autumn is a season that many people enjoy because it brings cooler weather and a calm, comfy atmosphere after the hot summer. The changing colors of the leaves make the environment very beautiful, and wearing sweaters and hoodies adds to the comfort of the season. Autumn is also loved for its holidays and relaxing evenings.

Dislikes

However, the season also has its downsides, such as shorter days, colder mornings, and frequent rain that can feel gloomy. For some people, just the thought of the transition into winter makes them feel down. The worst of it is when you have a lot of trees in your hard. Raking the fallens leaves is not fun.

Winter

Likes

Even though many people don't like the winter, it certainly has its unique charm. The snowy landscapes, the snow that build on tree on tree branches is absolutly beautiful. The cold weather makes staying indoors feel cozy, especially with warm blankets, hot chocolate, and time spent with family during holidays like Christmas and New Year.

Dislikes

Winter can be difficult because of freezing temperatures, slippery roads, and shorter days that make everything feel darker and more tiring. Getting out of bed on cold mornings is hard, and illnesses like colds and flu are more common. The snow build up makes driving on roads an absolute nightmare.

Spring

Likes

Spring is a refreshing season that many people enjoy because it represents new beginnings and fresh energy. Flowers bloom, trees turn green again, and the warmer weather makes it pleasant to spend time outdoors. Spring is also a great time for picnics, festivals, and enjoying longer days.

Dislikes

However, the season has its downsides, including unpredictable weather, sudden rain showers, and allergies caused by pollen. Insects also start to appear, which can be annoying. Daylights saving ruining my sleep.
